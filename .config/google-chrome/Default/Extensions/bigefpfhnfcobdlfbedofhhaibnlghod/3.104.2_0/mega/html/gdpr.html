European General Data Protection Regulation

On 25 May 2018, the European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) came into effect. The GDPR provides rights to European users, but, as a leading privacy company, we make the GDPR protections and rights available to all our users globally in respect of personal data (or “personal information” as we refer to it in New Zealand), wherever they may live.

Mega’s Terms of Service, Privacy & Data Policy, Takedown Guidance and Contacts provide all the disclosures and explanations required by the GDPR.
